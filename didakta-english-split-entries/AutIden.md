# §AutIden. Identicalness[^130]

After the article, in the attributive position, [αὐτός](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=au%29to%2Fs&la=greek&can=au%29to%2Fs10&prior=au\)to/s) in any case means same. Thus, [ὁ](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=o%28&la=greek&can=o%283&prior=au\)to/s) [αὐτὸς](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=au%29to%5Cs&la=greek&can=au%29to%5Cs3&prior=o\() [ἀνήρ](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=a%29nh%2Fr&la=greek&can=a%29nh%2Fr1&prior=au\)to\\s), rarely ([ὁ](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=o%28&la=greek&can=o%284&prior=a\)nh/r)) [ἀνὴρ](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=a%29nh%5Cr&la=greek&can=a%29nh%5Cr1&prior=o\() [ὁ](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=o%28&la=greek&can=o%285&prior=a\)nh\\r) [αὐτός](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=au%29to%2Fs&la=greek&can=au%29to%2Fs11&prior=o\() the same man. 

→ “[τοῦ](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=tou%3D&la=greek&can=tou%3D3&prior=au\)to/s) [**αὐτοῦ**](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=au%29tou%3D&la=greek&can=au%29tou%3D3&prior=tou=) [θέρους](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=qe%2Frous&la=greek&can=qe%2Frous0&prior=au\)tou=)” [**T. 4.58**](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/text?doc=Thuc.%204.58&lang=original); ⇾ in the same summer

→ “[τὰ](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=ta%5C&la=greek&can=ta%5C0&prior=qe/rous) [**αὐτὰ**](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=au%29ta%5C&la=greek&can=au%29ta%5C1&prior=ta\\) [ταῦτα](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=tau%3Dta&la=greek&can=tau%3Dta0&prior=au\)ta\\)” [**X. A. 1.1.7**](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/text?doc=Xen.%20Anab.%201.1.7&lang=original); ⇾ these same things

→ “[οἱ](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=oi%28&la=greek&can=oi%280&prior=tau=ta) [τοὺς](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=tou%5Cs&la=greek&can=tou%5Cs0&prior=oi\() [**αὐτοὺς**](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=au%29tou%5Cs&la=greek&can=au%29tou%5Cs0&prior=tou\\s) [αἰεὶ](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=ai%29ei%5C&la=greek&can=ai%29ei%5C0&prior=au\)tou\\s) [περὶ](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=peri%5C&la=greek&can=peri%5C0&prior=ai\)ei\\) [τῶν](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=tw%3Dn&la=greek&can=tw%3Dn0&prior=peri\\) [**αὐτῶν**](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=au%29tw%3Dn&la=greek&can=au%29tw%3Dn0&prior=tw=n) [λόγους](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=lo%2Fgous&la=greek&can=lo%2Fgous0&prior=au\)tw=n) [λέγοντες](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=le%2Fgontes&la=greek&can=le%2Fgontes0&prior=lo/gous)” [**Ant. 5.50**](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/text?doc=Antiph.%205.50&lang=original); ⇾ the people who are continually making the same speeches about the same things

→ “[ἐγὼ](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=e%29gw%5C&la=greek&can=e%29gw%5C0&prior=le/gontes) [μὲν](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=me%5Cn&la=greek&can=me%5Cn0&prior=e\)gw\\) [ὁ](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=o%28&la=greek&can=o%286&prior=me\\n) [**αὐτός**](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=au%29to%2Fs&la=greek&can=au%29to%2Fs12&prior=o\() [εἰμι](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=ei%29mi&la=greek&can=ei%29mi0&prior=au\)to/s), [ἱμεῖς](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=i%28_mei%3Ds&la=greek&can=i%28_mei%3Ds0&prior=ei\)mi) [δὲ](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=de%5C&la=greek&can=de%5C1&prior=i\(_mei=s) [μεταβάλλετε](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=metaba%2Fllete&la=greek&can=metaba%2Fllete0&prior=de\\)” [**T. 2.61**](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/text?doc=Thuc.%202.61&lang=original); ⇾ I am the same, it is you who change

With a proper name or a word denoting an individual, the article is omitted
