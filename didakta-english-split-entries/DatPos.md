# §DatPos. Possessor[^24]

Dative denotes possession, belonging, or interest with verbs such as [γίγνομαι](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=gi%2Fgnomai&la=greek&can=gi%2Fgnomai0&prior=misqo/n) and [εἰμί](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=ei%29mi%2F&la=greek&can=ei%29mi%2F0&prior=gi/gnomai). The person for whom a thing exists is put in the dative.
