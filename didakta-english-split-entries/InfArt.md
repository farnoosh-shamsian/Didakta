# §InfArt. Articular Infinitive[^115]

The infinitive in combination with a neuter singular article can function as a noun phrase. The articular infinitive, while having the character of a noun, retains the functions of a verb. The articular infinitive can be used similar to any other noun phrase and admits the constructions of an ordinary noun. It may indicate time (after verbs of saying or thinking), or may be timeless. The case is indicated by the article:
