# §MidSpee. Speech Act Middle[^88]

Verbs expressing certain kinds of speech are often middle-only. Most speech act middle verbs have a rather specific lexical meaning, while more general or neutral verbs of saying tend to be active.

The subject of a speech act middle could be involved in the speech act in different ways, for example, as a beneficiary or an experiencer. Some middle verbs of speech may indicate a strong emotional or mental involvement of the subject.
