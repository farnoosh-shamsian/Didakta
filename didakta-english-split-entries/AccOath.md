# §AccOath. Oaths[^45]

The accusative of oath indicates the person or thing by which one swears and is accompanied bythe adverbs of swearing such as [μά](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=ma%2F&la=greek&can=ma%2F0&prior=qew=n), [οὐ](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=ou%29&la=greek&can=ou%290&prior=ma/) [μά](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=ma%2F&la=greek&can=ma%2F1&prior=ou\)), [ναὶ](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=nai%5C&la=greek&can=nai%5C0&prior=ma/) [μά](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=ma%2F&la=greek&can=ma%2F2&prior=nai\\), [νή](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=nh%2F&la=greek&can=nh%2F0&prior=ma/), or verbs of swearing, [ὀμνύναι](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=o%29mnu%2Fnai&la=greek&can=o%29mnu%2Fnai0) swear by ([τοὺς](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=tou%5Cs&la=greek&can=tou%5Cs0&prior=o\)mnu/nai) [θεούς](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=qeou%2Fs&la=greek&can=qeou%2Fs0&prior=tou\\s), pass. [Ζεὺς](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=*zeu%5Cs&la=greek&can=*zeu%5Cs0&prior=qeou/s) [ὀμώμοται](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=o%29mw%2Fmotai&la=greek&can=o%29mw%2Fmotai0&prior=*zeu\\s)) and *swear to* ([τὸν](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=to%5Cn&la=greek&can=to%5Cn0&prior=o\)mw/motai) [ὅρκον](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=o%28%2Frkon&la=greek&can=o%28%2Frkon0&prior=to\\n), pass. [ὁ](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=o%28&la=greek&can=o%280&prior=o\(/rkon) [ὅρκος](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=o%28%2Frkos&la=greek&can=o%28%2Frkos0&prior=o\() [ὀμώμοται](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=o%29mw%2Fmotai&la=greek&can=o%29mw%2Fmotai1&prior=o\(/rkos)).

→ “[ὀμνύω](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=o%29mnu%2Fw&la=greek&can=o%29mnu%2Fw0&prior=ma/) [ὑμῖν](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=u%28mi%3Dn&la=greek&can=u%28mi%3Dn0&prior=o\)mnu/w) [**θεοὺς**](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=qeou%5Cs&la=greek&can=qeou%5Cs0&prior=u\(mi=n) **[πάντας](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=pa%2Fntas&la=greek&can=pa%2Fntas0&prior=qeou\\s) [καὶ](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=kai%5C&la=greek&can=kai%5C0&prior=pa/ntas) [πάσας](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=pa%2Fsas&la=greek&can=pa%2Fsas0&prior=kai\\)**” [**Xen. Anab. 6.1.31**](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/text?doc=Xen.%20Anab.%206.1.31&lang=original); ⇾ I swear to you by all the gods and goddesses.

[μά](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=ma%2F&la=greek&can=ma%2F3&prior=*di/a) is negative, except when preceded by [ναί](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=nai%2F&la=greek&can=nai%2F0&prior=ma/). [μά](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=ma%2F&la=greek&can=ma%2F4&prior=nai/) may stand alone when a negative precedes (often in a question) or when a negative follows in the next clause: 

→ “[μὰ](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=ma%5C&la=greek&can=ma%5C3&prior=ma/) [**τὸν**](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=to%5Cn&la=greek&can=to%5Cn5&prior=ma\\) **[Ἀπόλλω](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=*%29apo%2Fllw&la=greek&can=*%29apo%2Fllw0&prior=to\\n)**, [οὔκ](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=ou%29%2Fk&la=greek&can=ou%29%2Fk0&prior=*\)apo/llw)” [**Ar. Thesm. 269**](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/text?doc=Aristoph.%20Thes.%20269&lang=original); ⇾ No, by Apollo

[μά](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=ma%2F&la=greek&can=ma%2F5&prior=ou\)/k) is sometimes omitted after [οὐ](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=ou%29&la=greek&can=ou%292&prior=ma/), and after [ναί](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=nai%2F&la=greek&can=nai%2F1&prior=ou\)): 

→ “[οὐ](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=ou%29&la=greek&can=ou%293&prior=nai/) [**τὸν**](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=to%5Cn&la=greek&can=to%5Cn6&prior=ou\)) **[Ὄλυμπον](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=*%29%2Folumpon&la=greek&can=*%29%2Folumpon0&prior=to\\n)**” [**S. O. T. 1088**](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/text?doc=Soph.%20OT%201088&lang=original); ⇾ by heaven, \[you wil\] not.
