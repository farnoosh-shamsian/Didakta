# §AccOath. سوێند

دۆخی ئاکووزاتیڤ له ڕێکەوەندەکانی سوێند خواردن لەگەڵ ئاوەڵ‌کاری سوێند وه‌کوو [μά](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=ma%2F&la=greek&can=ma%2F0&prior=qew=n), [οὐ](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=ou%29&la=greek&can=ou%290&prior=ma/) [μά](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=ma%2F&la=greek&can=ma%2F1&prior=ou\)), [ναὶ](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=nai%5C&la=greek&can=nai%5C0&prior=ma/) [μά](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=ma%2F&la=greek&can=ma%2F2&prior=nai%5C), [νή](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=nh%2F&la=greek&can=nh%2F0&prior=ma/) یان کردای سوێند وه‌کوو [ὀμνύναι](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=o%29mnu%2Fnai&la=greek&can=o%29mnu%2Fnai0) بەکارده‌هێنرێت و دەربڕی به که‌س یان شتێکە که سوێندی پێ دەخۆن.  
→ “[ὀμνύω](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=o%29mnu%2Fw&la=greek&can=o%29mnu%2Fw0&prior=ma/) [ὑμῖν](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=u%28mi%3Dn&la=greek&can=u%28mi%3Dn0&prior=o\)mnu/w) [**θεοὺς**](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=qeou%5Cs&la=greek&can=qeou%5Cs0&prior=u\(mi=n) **[πάντας](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=pa%2Fntas&la=greek&can=pa%2Fntas0&prior=qeou%5Cs) [καὶ](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=kai%5C&la=greek&can=kai%5C0&prior=pa/ntas) [πάσας](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=pa%2Fsas&la=greek&can=pa%2Fsas0&prior=kai%5C)**” [**Xen. Anab. 6.1.31**](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/text?doc=Xen.%20Anab.%206.1.31&lang=original); ⇾   
«سوێندت ده‌دەم بە هەموو خواژنه‌کان و خوداوەندەکان»   
زۆرجار [μά](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=ma%2F&la=greek&can=ma%2F3&prior=*di/a) بۆ نەویستن[^89]، بەرپەرچ‌دانەوە یان ڕەت‌دانەوە به‌کاردێت، مەگەر ئەوەی‌کە [ναί](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=nai%2F&la=greek&can=nai%2F0&prior=ma/) لە پێش‌دا هاتبێت. ئه‌گه‌ر پیتی نەویستن پێش [μά](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=ma%2F&la=greek&can=ma%2F3&prior=*di/a) هاتبێت یان له دەستەواژەی دواتردا هاتبێت، [μά](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=ma%2F&la=greek&can=ma%2F3&prior=*di/a) ده‌توانێ به ته‌نێ به‌کاربێت.  
→ “[μὰ](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=ma%5C&la=greek&can=ma%5C3&prior=ma/) [**τὸν**](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=to%5Cn&la=greek&can=to%5Cn5&prior=ma%5C) **[Ἀπόλλω](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=*%29apo%2Fllw&la=greek&can=*%29apo%2Fllw0&prior=to%5Cn)**, [οὔκ](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=ou%29%2Fk&la=greek&can=ou%29%2Fk0&prior=*\)apo/llw)” [**Ar. Thesm. 269**](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/text?doc=Aristoph.%20Thes.%20269&lang=original); ⇾   
«نە، سوێند به ئاپۆلۆن»  
هەندێک جار ئه‌گه‌ر [μά](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=ma%2F&la=greek&can=ma%2F3&prior=*di/a) له دوای [οὐ](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=ou%29&la=greek&can=ou%292&prior=ma/) یان [ναί](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=nai%2F&la=greek&can=nai%2F1&prior=ou\)) هاتبێت، ده سڕدرێتەوە:  
→ “[οὐ](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=ou%29&la=greek&can=ou%293&prior=nai/) [**τὸν**](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=to%5Cn&la=greek&can=to%5Cn6&prior=ou\)) **[Ὄλυμπον](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=*%29%2Folumpon&la=greek&can=*%29%2Folumpon0&prior=to%5Cn)**” [**S. O. T. 1088**](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/text?doc=Soph.%20OT%201088&lang=original); ⇾   
« سوێند به ئوله‌مپ نه»
