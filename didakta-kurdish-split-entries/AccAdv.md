# §AccAdv. ئاوەڵکار

زۆرێک لە ناۆه‌ ئاکووزاتیڤه‌کان وەکو ئاوەڵکار[^87] کاردەکەن و مانای کردارەکان سنووردار دەکەن.  
دەربڕینی چۆنیەتی ئەنجامدانی کار: [τρόπον](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=tro%2Fpon&la=greek&can=tro%2Fpon0&prior=ploutei=n) [τινά](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=tina%2F&la=greek&can=tina%2F0&prior=tro/pon) به چەشنێک، [τίνα](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=ti%2Fna&la=greek&can=ti%2Fna0&prior=tina/) [τρόπον](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=tro%2Fpon&la=greek&can=tro%2Fpon1&prior=ti/na) بە چه شێوەیەک؟، [τόνδε](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=to%2Fnde&la=greek&can=to%2Fnde0&prior=tro/pon) ([τοῦτον](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=tou%3Dton&la=greek&can=tou%3Dton0&prior=to/nde)) [τὸν](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=to%5Cn&la=greek&can=to%5Cn2&prior=tou=ton) [τρόπον](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=tro%2Fpon&la=greek&can=tro%2Fpon2&prior=to%5Cn) بەم شێوەیە، [πάντα](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=pa%2Fnta&la=greek&can=pa%2Fnta1&prior=tro/pon) [τρόπον](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=tro%2Fpon&la=greek&can=tro%2Fpon3&prior=pa/nta) بە هەر شێوەیەک، [τὴν](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=th%5Cn&la=greek&can=th%5Cn2&prior=tro/pw%7C) [ταχίστην](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=taxi%2Fsthn&la=greek&can=taxi%2Fsthn0&prior=th%5Cn)بە خێراترین شێواز، [τὴν](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=th%5Cn&la=greek&can=th%5Cn3&prior=o\(do/n) [εὐθεῖαν](http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/morph?l=eu%29qei%3Dan&la=greek&can=eu%29qei%3Dan0&prior=th%5Cn) ڕاستەوخۆ، یەک‌ڕاست.
